About Andromeda

its andromeda

A few years ago, my friend and I would find as much pretty leaves as possible. Once we were satisfied with our collection, we would bring them to her house. With some wax paper and a clothing iron, we would place the leaves in between two sheets and heat the paper with the iron, making a sheet of now wax-preserved leaves. I usually left the finished item alone, but my friend would like to take them out and hold the now-preserved leaves.

I'll keep looking for some leaves.
